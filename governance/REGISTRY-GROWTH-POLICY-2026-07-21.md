# Registry growth policy (census, not quota)

> status: FIXED 2026-07-21 (8-voice adversarial council over MEASURED GitHub
> flow — dedup across 10 AI topics and the full-GitHub universe, 12-month and
> 90-day windows, bars 200/500/1000★; one voice DNF, waived and recorded).
> The REGISTRY (site cards + commit/citation axes) is decoupled from the
> frozen dependents-axis PANEL (which catches up quarterly by value-blind
> manifest). This policy governs the registry only. Execution of the initial
> backlog wave: the August 2026 monthly ritual.

## Principles (council 7/7)

1. **Census, not quota.** The institute never chooses systems and never sets
   an intake number. It publishes an entry BAR and a mechanical PREDICATE;
   every qualifier enters automatically. The monthly rate is an OUTPUT.
   (Both debated numbers — "20-40/month" and "100-200/week" — were rejected
   as category errors.)
2. **Falling behind is impossible by construction**: everything above the bar
   is in. Only a discretionary sample can fall behind; this policy abolishes
   discretion.
3. **Entry is an obligation, not a prize.** Star-gaming is not fought with
   discretionary exclusions; a pumped repo simply exhibits its flat series in
   public. No per-case overrides, ever.

## The rule (AI-v1)

- **Public bar: 500 GitHub stars**, observed at a monthly census over the
  FULL GitHub ≥500★ universe (topic tags are classification features, never
  the discovery boundary). Once admitted, never removed for star decline.
- **Mandate predicate (mechanical, versioned):** public repo · not a fork ·
  not archived · OSS license from a frozen allowlist · first-party code
  outside docs/examples (not Markdown-only) · (≥1 release/package OR ≥50
  commits in trailing 12 months) · name/description/topics do not match the
  frozen non-system blocklist (awesome|tutorial|course|roadmap|cheatsheet|
  list|book|interview|mirror|weights) · RESCUE: a borderline repo enters
  anyway if ≥10 commits in trailing 90 days AND ≥3 distinct contributors in
  trailing 12 months. Predicate amendments = a new dated version re-run
  retroactively against the whole universe; never a case decision.
- **Cards index after 4 weekly observations**; before that, admitted systems
  are visible in a machine-readable pending manifest (membership is complete
  and auditable from day one; thin-content GEO risk avoided).
- **Shadow layer (non-public): 200-499★ candidates** get minimal weekly
  observation (star counts only) — early point-in-time raw material for
  future private data products; never published, never a card.
- **Expected flow: ~55 systems/month (range 36-72), capacity 75, uncapped.**
  The first 6 monthly censuses measure the true crossing flow (the initial
  estimates are stocks with right-censoring); forecasts adapt, the rule does
  not.

## Backlog (initial wave)

- 2026-07-31: hash-anchored census of the full existing stock ≥500★.
- The pending manifest of every mechanically eligible system is published
  IMMEDIATELY (completeness of membership precedes card maturity).
- Activation in deterministic order (repository ID ascending — no prominence
  ordering), complete within ≤12 months; forward crossings take priority from
  2026-08-01 so new coverage debt never accumulates.
- Backlog cards enter with their FULL history from repo creation (commit +
  citation series) — the registry's GEO anchor, not its burden.

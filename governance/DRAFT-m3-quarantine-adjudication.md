## Steel-man of the alternative (block m3 until the spine locks)

The sequence rule §4 was not pedantry: an axis added pre-spine bakes its schema
assumptions into the live series before the data model is final, and every such graft
narrows the spine's freedom (the panel rejected exactly this move 5/5 when MiniMax
proposed it). Discipline about locked artifacts IS the institute's product; overriding
a lock 12 days after locking it teaches future sessions that locks are soft. If the
spine re-spar lands in August, waiting costs one month of deps series — recoverable
via shadow capture — while a wrong axis contract on the live line is append-only debt.

## Position (recommendation to the keeper)

**Confirm m3 promotion on the live line, gated by the quarantine funnel — and
simultaneously amend §4 forward, replacing the calendar-sequence rule with a
process rule.** Reasons, in order of weight:

1. **The gate vacuum is a measured, present harm.** Only a small minority of tracked
   systems are even gate-capable on two axes (axis-2 present = 23/134 after the
   2026-07-10 relink); the convergence gate — the institute's namesake product —
   cannot fire without a third independent axis. Waiting for the spine keeps the
   product empty for months. Harm(waiting) is certain; harm(grafting) is hypothetical
   and mitigated below.
2. **The quarantine funnel already provides the protection §4 wanted.** Invariant
   filter → shadow capture (running since 2026-07-01) → pre-registered A/B criteria →
   human gate. What §4 actually guards against is an UNVETTED axis contaminating the
   canon; the funnel vets. The calendar rule ("after the spine") was a proxy for the
   real requirement ("through a vetting process"); replace the proxy with the
   requirement.
3. **Shadow capture is append-only insurance either way.** The deps series is being
   captured regardless of the decision; promotion only changes whether it SCORES.
   Retroactive de-scoring (methodology version bump) is cheap; retroactive capture is
   impossible (I5).
4. **The spine keeps its authority where it is load-bearing.** The amendment does not
   abolish the spine gate for SCHEMA changes — only for axis ADMISSION. The spine still
   locks the data model; m3 must conform to whatever the spine locks (m3's contract is
   explicitly re-basable, per the quarantine doc's methodology-version discipline).

**Conditions attached to the confirmation (make the override cost visible):**
- The §4 amendment is a dated forward append to COLLECT-V3-SPEC (never a rewrite),
  cross-referencing this adjudication.
- m3 promotion happens only if its pre-registered A/B criteria pass, and enters as a
  new methodology version (m3), never mutating m2 rows.

  invalidates the deps axis contract, m3 is demoted back to shadow by the same
